Mary Knight Dunlap

Mary Knight Dunlap is a women Veterinarian and a founder of the Association for Women Veterinarians. Dr. Mary Knight Dunlap recognized women's challenges in the male-dominated profession and sought to create a support system for them. So in 1947, she founded the Association for Women Veterinarians.
