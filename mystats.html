Chicken Biryani

Chicken biryani is known for its rich and aromatic flavors. It combines tender chicken pieces with fragrant basmati rice and a blend of spices, such as cumin, coriander, cardamom, and cinnamon. Chicken biryani is a favorite for me because of its incredible taste, versatility, cultural significance.
